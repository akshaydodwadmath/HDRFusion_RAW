This is the table of camera parameters from the dcraw source file, which were 
originally lifted from Adobe (they come here from the adobe_coeff() function 
in dcraw). They are presented  in the following format for each camera model:

{"Camera Model", <black>, <saturation>, {<9 ints>} }

These values for <black> and <saturation> are what dcraw uses to linearize 
the raw images. The array of 9 int values are the row-wise entries into the 
XYZ-to-Camera color space matrix for the camera model, multiplied by 1e4.
